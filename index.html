The Counterfeit Loop · A Field Guide

Love as a verb,
and its counterfeits.

The psychological and clinical literature converges: love is not a passive feeling, but an active choice. When we conflate emotional investment (cathexis) or self-stabilization with love, we construct counterfeits where the other person is used to fill our own deficits.

The Turning Point

Abundance before the offer.

Every counterfeit is an attempt to extract from a person what is missing in ourselves — safety, worth, peace, justification. That is why it deforms into control or obsession. We hand a human the weight only a transcendent source of grace was meant to carry, crushing both them and ourselves.

Love cannot be willed out of starvation. The cure is not simply "trying harder" to act selflessly. It is being filled at the source first, so the other is released from being your savior and can finally be received as a gift.

Filled at the source → the other freed from carrying it → love that gives without invoicing
